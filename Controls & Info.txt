Space Zero version 0.8.5

W - move forward
S - Slow down/Stop
A - rotate left
D - rotate right

Left Shift + W - Boost foward at higher speed (uses fuel)

Left Click - Shoot
Right Click - Hold to lock onto enemies and release to shoot missiles at enemies locked on

ESC - Quit the game

Move the cursor around to look around and change direction.
Fuel replenishes automatically while you don't use it
Arrows at the edge of the screen will guide you towards enemies to destroy
Destroy all enemies in a wave and proceed to the next one
